Do you think you have what it takes to be King of the Blue Hills?

The King of The Blue Hills Competion will run from January 1st, 2015 through October 31st, 2015. We will be tracking club member personal records between January and October on the gnarliest climbs in and around the Blue Hills Reservation. Points will be awarded to the top 10 times and weighted based on the difficutly of the climb.

Categories

  • Men Open
  • Women Open
  • Dirt Open

Rules and Eligibility

  • Must be a current member of the Blue Hills Cycling Club. Not a member? Join now on Bikereg.
  • Join the Blue Hills Cycling Club on Strava.
  • All climbs must be completed between January 1st, through October 31st, 2015.
  • Segment Leaders and overall Leaders will be tracked on the following Page.

Monthly Challenges

Every month from March through October we will be tracking club member mileage, total elevation gain and hours on the bike. At the end of each month we will recognize a winner of each category. We may throw in a few more categories to mix it up during the season.

Rules and Eligibility

  • Must be a current member of the Blue Hills Cycling Club. Not a member? Join now on Bikereg.
  • Club Officers may participate, but are not eligble for prizes.
  • You can only win one category per month and only win that category once.(Feel free to blow the mileage out of the park everymonth, we are just going to give a prize to the next guy)
  • You must grant permission to the Blue Hills Cycling Club Leaderboard Application by clicking the Log in With Strava button below
