BHCC is a growing club, now with well over 100 active members of all disciplines and abilities, including accomplished road, mountain bike and cyclocross racers, triathletes, century and charity riders, weekend warriors, and many riding solely for fitness. Our experienced riders are generous with advice and encouragement.

Membership runs the calendar year; dues are $25 per person. Club members will receive weekly emails with scheduled rides, club social events and other information. Club members also receive a generous discount at Landry's Bicycles.

To join BHCC simply click the BikeReg logo.
